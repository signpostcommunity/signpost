Know what's happening

Track Your Requests

Tired of constantly worrying that your interpreter request hasn't been submitted, or wondering if they booked someone who isn't a good fit? On signpost, you are tagged in requests for your appointments and get full visibility the whole way through with the Interpreter Booking Tracker.

See Who's Been Contacted — Expand any request to see which interpreters have been contacted, who has replied, and who is confirmed. Never be surprised by someone from your "No" list turning up at your doctor appointment.

Community

Filter based on your Trusted Circle's specific recommendations: by skill set, professionalism, location, and more.

Communicate your way

Video capabilities: Add information in written English or ASL

Introduce Yourself — Share a little about yourself (either in writing or in ASL), and it gets saved to your profile. Your interpreters can learn the basics about you in advance, so they can do the best possible job for you, from the minute you meet.

Appointment-Specific Videos — Have a big presentation coming up at work? Need to make sure the interpreter knows specialized signs for your physics class? Include a video in your interpreter request to give them context in ASL. You choose whether they see it before or after being confirmed.

Your preferences. Your control.

Interpreter Directory

Browse real interpreter profiles with photos, intro videos, credentials, specializations. Filter by language, location, certification, and more. It's time to choose your interpreter, instead of someone else choosing for you.

Build Your Preferred List — Nobody sees your full interpreter list unless you share it. Save interpreters to Preferred and Secondary tiers, set which contexts you want them for (work/medical/personal), and share with your requesters so they always know who to call first.

Do Not Book — Keep a private list of interpreters you'd prefer not to work with. Shared with your requesters so you're never booked with someone who isn't a fit.

Your preferences travel with you

Share Your Interpreter Preferences

Your employer, doctor's office, school, or event coordinator can book interpreters for you on signpost. When they do, your Preferred list, communication preferences, and Do Not Book list automatically flow into their booking form.

Your Interpreter Request Link — Get a personal link and QR code. Show it to any coordinator on your phone, and they're connected to your preferences in seconds. One scan, and every future booking they make for you is informed by who you trust.

Full Visibility — Every booking made on your behalf shows up in your dashboard, tagged with who requested it. You get the same updates as if you had made the request yourself. No more wondering if the request was submitted or who they booked.

How it works
You're starting a new job. HR needs to book interpreters for your meetings.
  1. Open your signpost dashboard and show the QR code to your HR coordinator.
  2. They scan it and create a signpost account (or log in if they already have one).
  3. Your interpreter preferences automatically appear in their booking form.
  4. When they book an interpreter, your Preferred list is prioritized, and you get updates on every booking.
  5. If you leave the company, revoke the connection with one click. Your preferences stop flowing immediately.
Multiple Deaf participants? signpost finds the best fit for everyone.
When a coordinator is booking an interpreter for a meeting with several Deaf participants, signpost cross-references everyone's preferred lists to surface interpreters who work well for the whole group. The strongest matches are shown first, and anyone on someone's Do Not Book list is automatically flagged as "Not recommended for this request."
You control the connection. Always.
A coordinator only sees your preferences after you've approved the connection. You can revoke access at any time, and your Do Not Book list is always protected. Coordinators see flagged interpreters as "Not recommended for this request" with no explanation of whose list they're on or why.

Honest feedback, safely

Rate Your Interpreters (Confidential)

Public rating systems don't work in small, tight-knit communities. People often give artificially high ratings, or stay silent rather than risk a relationship. So we made ratings confidential. Yours are never shared with interpreters, and you can review them any time.

Your feedback does reach us, anonymously. signpost may use patterns across ratings to highlight quality interpreters or take action on interpreters with patterns of concerning feedback.
